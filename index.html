Our Services

Restaurant

Mitho restaurant is a classy and elegant Nepalese restaurant established in 1984. Our culinary philosophy is deceptively simple. We celebrate the very best of Nepalese food. Mitho cuisine is an extraordinary combination of flavours, delicate seasonings, in- house freshly ground spices, marinades, and complex tastes. We use only fresh and locally sourced ingredients.

Live Music & Bar

Come and relax in one of our plush armchairs and sample our signature cocktails, or enjoy our nice collection of wines and beers at the Mitho's Bar. Our passionate bar team can create a cocktail of your choice, or you may choose from the vast selection of classic cocktails on the menu.
Enjoy a crisp glass of Champagne or take advantage of our large selection of spirits that take pride of place.

Cafe

A TRENDY, INDEPENDENT CAFÉ IN THE CITY CENTRE
Whether you’re stopping by for a quick espresso or a refreshing cold brew - our coffee shop offers Birmingham’s coffee lovers a casual setting to fulfil caffeine cravings.
